# IPL Prodigy — Vaibhav Suryavanshi

A tribute landing page celebrating **Vaibhav Suryavanshi's** historic IPL 2026 season. At just 15 years old, he won the Orange Cap, smashed a world-record 72 sixes (breaking Chris Gayle's 14-year-old record of 59), and posted a jaw-dropping strike rate — sweeping a clutch of individual awards along the way.

This is my take on **The Odin Project's Landing Page** assignment.

## Live Demo

[https://sspatel107.github.io/ipl-prodigy-page/](https://sspatel107.github.io/ipl-prodigy-page/)

## What I Built

I built this page using only **HTML and CSS**, recreating the classic Odin Project landing page layout and then making it my own:

- **Hero section** with a navbar, headline, supporting copy, a call-to-action button, and an action shot of Vaibhav.
- **"5-Trophy Clean Sweep" cards** highlighting his Orange Cap, Most Sixes award, Super Striker title, and MVP & Emerging Player honours — each paired with a real photo.
- **Quote section** featuring Chris Gayle's actual reaction on X to Vaibhav breaking his record.
- **Call-to-action banner** and a **footer** to round things off.

## Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3 (Flexbox, custom properties, gradients)
- Google Fonts (Roboto)
